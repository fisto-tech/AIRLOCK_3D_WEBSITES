Instructions for Rotating, Scaling, and Panning a model using both mouse and touch gestures.

Rotating a Model

Using a Mouse:
Option 1 : Click and hold the left mouse button on the model. Move the mouse in the direction you want to rotate the model. Release the mouse button to stop the rotation.
Option 2 : Use the arrow keys (Left, Right, Up, Down) on the keyboard to rotate the model.
Using Touch:
Option 1 : Touch and hold the model with one finger. Move your finger in the direction you want to rotate the model. Lift your finger to stop rotating.

Scaling a Model

Using a Mouse:
Option 1 : Click and hold the middle mouse button on the model. Move the mouse in any direction to scale the model. Release the mouse button to stop scaling.
Using Touch:
Option 1 : Touch and hold the model with two fingers. Move your fingers in an up-and-down direction to scale the model. Lift your fingers to stop scaling.

Panning a Model

Using a Mouse:
Option 1 : Click and hold the right mouse button on the model. Move the mouse in any direction to pan the model. Release the mouse button to stop panning.
Using Touch:
Option 1 : Touch and hold the model with two fingers. Move your fingers away from or towards each other to increase or decrease the size of the model. Lift your fingers to stop scaling.
